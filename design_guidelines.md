# Kemet Services Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach**: Drawing inspiration from modern professional services and pharmaceutical industry leaders, focusing on trust-building and expertise demonstration through clean, authoritative design.

## Core Design Elements

### A. Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Light mode: Teal primary (174 94% 39%), charcoal text (220 9% 20%)
- Dark mode: Teal primary (174 84% 60%), light gray text (220 9% 85%)

**Supporting Colors:**
- Gray scale: 220 9% variations (10%, 30%, 50%, 70%, 90%)
- Success: 142 76% 36% (for certifications)
- Background: Pure white/dark charcoal

### B. Typography
- **Primary**: Inter (Google Fonts) - Clean, professional sans-serif for UI
- **Secondary**: Source Serif 4 - For headings and emphasis, adding pharmaceutical authority
- **Hierarchy**: 3xl/2xl for heroes, xl/lg for sections, base for body text

### C. Layout System
**Tailwind Spacing**: Consistent use of 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 24 units
- Sections: py-16/py-24 for major sections
- Components: p-6/p-8 for cards and containers  
- Elements: gap-4/gap-6 for grids, mb-4/mb-6 for text spacing

### D. Component Library

**Navigation**:
- Sticky header with teal background and white text
- Clean horizontal navigation with CTA buttons (WhatsApp, Contact)
- Mobile hamburger menu with smooth transitions

**Cards & Content**:
- Clean white cards with subtle shadows (shadow-sm)
- Rounded corners (rounded-lg) for modern feel
- Training cards with hover elevations
- Testimonial cards with professional styling

**Forms & Interactions**:
- Teal primary buttons with hover states
- Outline buttons with blur backgrounds when over images
- Form inputs with focus states matching brand teal

**Data Display**:
- Category filtering with pill-style buttons
- Grid layouts for training courses (responsive 1-3 columns)
- Professional testimonials carousel

### E. Animations
**Minimal & Professional**:
- Subtle fade-ins for sections on scroll
- Smooth hover transitions on cards (transform scale 1.02)
- Carousel smooth transitions
- No distracting animations - focus on content

## Content Strategy
**Professional Authority**: Emphasize credentials, certifications, and pharmaceutical expertise through visual hierarchy and strategic use of serif typography for key messaging.

**Trust Building**: Clean layouts with ample whitespace, professional photography placeholders, and clear contact information prominence.

## Images
**Large Hero Image**: Yes - Professional pharmaceutical/training environment with overlay text
**Training Cards**: Thumbnail images for each course category
**About Section**: Team/facility photos showing professional pharmaceutical environment
**Testimonials**: Professional headshots of satisfied clients

All images should convey pharmaceutical professionalism, modern training environments, and West African context when appropriate.
